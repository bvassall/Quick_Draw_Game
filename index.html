❓ HOW TO PLAY

— SURVIVAL GUIDE —

🎯 BASICS

👆
SWIPE MODE (DEFAULT)
Touch and drag away from your target, then release to fire. Like pulling a trigger — the bullet goes opposite your swipe. A dashed aim line shows where you'll shoot.
👆
TAP MODE (OPTIONAL)
Tap directly on an enemy to shoot them. Switch modes in Settings. Left side uses left gun, right side uses right gun.
💀
KILL BEFORE THEY SHOOT
Enemies appear on both sides and will shoot you after a short delay. Kill them before the timer runs out!
🎯
AIM FOR THE HEAD
Hitting the upper third of an enemy counts as a headshot — dealing 3× damage and bonus gold.

↕ DODGE

👆↕
SWIPE UP OR DOWN
Drag your finger vertically to move your player up or down. Essential for dodging dynamite throws! Release to snap back to center.

🔥 COMBOS & STREAKS

⚡
KILL STREAK
Consecutive kills build your streak. Every 3 kills increases your score multiplier (up to x10). Let the combo timer run out and it resets.
💰
SPEED BONUS
The faster you kill an enemy after they spawn, the more gold you earn. Watch for the speed rating: Perfect, Fast, OK, or Slow.
